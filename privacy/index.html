PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: 15 July 2026

The Tendring Democracy Project ("TDP", "we", "our" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

What information we collect

We may collect personal information that you choose to provide, including your name, email address, telephone number and any information you include when contacting us, signing up for updates or volunteering.

Our website may also collect limited technical information, such as your IP address and browser type, to help keep the site secure and understand how it is used.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • respond to your enquiries;
  • send updates that you have asked to receive;
  • organise meetings, campaigns and volunteers;
  • administer donations or memberships, if applicable;
  • meet our legal obligations.

We will only use your personal information where we have a lawful basis to do so under UK data protection law.

Sharing your information

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

We may share it with trusted organisations that help us operate our website or communicate with supporters, or where we are required to do so by law.

Keeping your information

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is needed for the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You have the right to ask for a copy of the personal information we hold about you, to ask us to correct inaccurate information, or to request that we delete your information where appropriate.

If you have any concerns about how we use your personal information, please contact us. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we use your personal information, please contact us using the contact details on this website.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
